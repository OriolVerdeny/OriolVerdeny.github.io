Short Bio

I am a biologist with a background on biostatistics and interests in software development and web technologies. Nowadays I work in the interface between biology and data science addressing diverse problems related to insect mass-rearing, but I am also interested in a broad range of subjects from the field of applied biology such as pest management, agroecology and soil science.
